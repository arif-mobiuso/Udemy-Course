The Best Movies According to Arif

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Bahubali

"Baahubali: The Beginning" and its sequel "Baahubali: The Conclusion" are epic fantasy films directed by S. S. Rajamouli. The story follows Shivudu, who discovers his royal lineage in the kingdom of Mahishmati, leading to political intrigue, family betrayal, and a quest for justice. The films are known for their grand scale, impressive visuals, and international success.

Drishyam

"Drishyam" in Telugu, directed by Jeethu Joseph and starring Venkatesh Daggubati, is a suspenseful thriller that revolves around a common man, Rambabu, who becomes entangled in a web of deception to protect his family after an unexpected incident. Praised for its gripping narrative and strong performances, the Telugu version of "Drishyam" received acclaim within the Telugu film industry and from audiences.

kgf

"KGF: Chapter 1" is an action-drama film directed by Prashanth Neel, featuring Yash as Rocky, an orphan aspiring to become a powerful figure in the Kolar Gold Fields. The movie gained acclaim for its intense action, Yash's performance, and high production values, becoming a major success in the Indian film industry.
